OpenMeetings Call For Logo

As you may know, Apache Openmeetings is part of the Apache Software Foundation, which is a non-profit organization. In order to celebrate The Apache Software Foundation's 15th Anniversary and promote the ASF's more than 200 projects, Apache has launched an effort to get folks who use Apache projects to note that by including one or more of the newly-designed "Powered By Apache" logos on their websites, documentation, marketing materials, etc.

Guidelines for use are available here: http://apache.org/foundation/press/kit

Unfortunately we have no suitable logo for this :(

This page is designed to test all proposed logo and choose the best one

Please send your logos and opinions to user@ mail list (you need to subscribe first)
Thanks in advance :)
